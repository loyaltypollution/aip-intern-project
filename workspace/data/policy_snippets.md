# Municipal Feedback Response Policy

## SLA Requirements
- HIGH urgency: acknowledge within 4 hours, resolve within 48 hours
- MEDIUM urgency: acknowledge within 24 hours, resolve within 5 business days
- LOW urgency: acknowledge within 3 business days, resolve within 30 days

## Urgency Escalation Criteria
HIGH: safety risk to persons, infrastructure failure affecting multiple residents
MEDIUM: quality-of-life impact, policy violations, recurring complaints
LOW: general enquiries, cosmetic issues, single-occurrence noise complaints

## Category Owners
| Category | Department |
|----------|-----------|
| Infrastructure | Public Works Dept |
| Noise & Nuisance | Community Standards |
| Parks & Recreation | Parks & Leisure Dept |
| Public Safety | City Safety Office |

## PII Handling
Flag any message containing names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
Redact before publishing triage reports.

## Response Tone
Professional, empathetic, action-oriented. Avoid passive voice.
